EFIRM EGFR Instructions

EGFR-KIT-V1 (T790M, L858R, Exon19 Deletion Mutations)

Overview of Performing EFIRM



EFIRM is a easy to use 96-well plate assay. Please consult your specific SOP developed for your laboratory or call EZLife Bio if you have questions about the specific reagents and your assay. The following video shows the basic usage operations.


Protocol Information




Please refer to the most recent version of the Electric Field Induced Release and Measurement EGFR Test (L858R, Exon19Deletion, T790M) in Plasma and Saliva prepared through your home institution.

Documentation control should include: [1] Protocol operation, [2] EFIRM Instrument instruction manual, [3] Plate Washer instructional manual, [4] Validation plan.
