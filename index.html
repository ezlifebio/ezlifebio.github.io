EFIRM

Rethinking molecular diagnostics

Accurate. Non-invasive. Rapid

EFIRM (electric field induced release and measurement) is EZLife Bio's core for performing accurate disease testing. EFIRM is robust, rapid, PCR-free,and sample extraction free: electrochemical biosensors are effectively leveraged to streamline testing.

Only a drop (20 uL) of blood or saliva is needed to achieve nearly perfect detection of targets in as little as 30 minutes. As a company, EZLife Bio is poised to reshape and radically simplify how testing is done.

Read more about the science behind EFIRM

EFIRM: A complete system for molecular detection

EZLife Bio's system offers a complete solution from sample to answer. The Oasis Saliva Collector allows for rapid, non-invasive collection of saliva. Saliva can then be placed in the EFIRM96 Device for rapid detection of biomarkers. Finally, this data can be uploaded to the EZLife EasyData platform for rapid data and result reporting.
